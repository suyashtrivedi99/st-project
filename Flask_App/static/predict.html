About our Predictor

Malaria is a common and life-threatening disease in many tropical and subtropical areas. There are currently over 100 countries and territories where there is a risk of malaria transmission, and these are visited by more than 125 million international travellers every year. Each year many international travellers fall ill with malaria while visiting countries/territories where malaria is endemic, and well over 10 000 are reported to become ill with malaria after returning home; however, underreporting means that the real figure may be considerably higher.

Keeping in mind the seriousness of Malaria, we have made a predictor which predicts infection in cells by the Malarial parasite.
